# Personal Knowledge Base

A living, LLM-maintained wiki that grows with every source ingested and every question explored.

## How It Works

This wiki follows the [[LLM Wiki Pattern]] — instead of retrieving from raw documents at query time, knowledge is **compiled once and kept current**. Every new source gets integrated into existing pages, cross-references are maintained automatically, and the synthesis reflects everything accumulated so far.
